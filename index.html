Features

Digital Recording

Notes written in a paper will be recorded digitally in the device connected(mobile) with the pen.Connection will be through Zigbee,because of its low power consumption.

Hidden Camera

Hidden camera is installed in the tip of the pen which is very difficult to determine that camera is existing. Audio and video recording. Lens array of just 120 micrometers wide inside its casing and can focus from as close as 3mm away. 12MP camera with 720p resolution.Camera chip system is made up of silicon material so that it will consume less power and high resolution video. Recorded data is stored in the chip and can be transferred into system connected with the pen.

Audio Recorder

Separate audio recorder is installed in the pen, in case someone wants only to record audio.It has audio pickup range of 90 ft and recorded audio is stored in the chip and can be transferred to connected device.

Flexible Use

Pen can be used both in writing and using it as stylus.As per the required need writing or stylus we have to turn the lower part of the pen and it will function accordingly.

PPT Controller

There will be a button at the end of the clip which will act as a switch for slide change as well as switch for turning on and off of laser light installed in the finial of the pen.

Security

To increase security and avoid from breaching of data only one device can be connected at a time through wireless medium .

Wireless Charging

Wireless charging,Pen will be charged by an induction station.
